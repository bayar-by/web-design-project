Essential Food Safety Practices

Ensuring food safety is crucial to prevent foodborne illnesses. Learn about proper food handling, storage, and preparation techniques.

Safe Cooking Temperatures

Meat Cooking Guidelines

Understanding the correct internal temperatures for different types of meat to ensure they are safely cooked.

Cleanliness

Regularly washing hands, surfaces, and utensils to prevent the spread of bacteria and other pathogens.

Safe Storage

Refrigerating or freezing perishables, prepared foods, and leftovers within two hours (one hour if the temperature is above 90°F). Ensuring the refrigerator is at or below 40°F (4°C) and the freezer is at or below 0°F (-18°C).
